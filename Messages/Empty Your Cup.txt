You have unlocked the ability to Empty Your Cup.
This will reset your current progress and allow
you to pick a school of Martial Arts again.

If you select the same school, though you forget
everything you learned, residual memory will 
allow you to gain 50% more experience as you practice.

If you select a different school, your enthusiam for
diversify and mastering all Martial Arts will allow
you to learn skills with 10% less energy.

Navigate to the Cup of Knowledge tab and click on
Empty Your Cup. Click Are You Sure to confirm.


“Empty your cup so that it may be filled; become 
devoid to gain totality.”
