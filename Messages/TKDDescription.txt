Taekwondo is a Korean form of martial arts characterized by punching and kicking techniques, 
with emphasis on head-height kicks, spinning jump kicks, and fast kicking techniques. The literal 
translation for tae kwon do is "kicking," "punching," and "the art or way of."

Pros:
- Kicks generate 50% more Attack
- All Special attacks are available after learning the first kick.

Cons:
- Generate 10% less defense
